About Us

Established in the heart of Guntur, Anu Boutique has been at the forefront of artistic embroidery, tailoring, and maggam work for over a decade.

Known for impeccable craftsmanship and creative, personalized designs, we specialize in:

  • Custom Tailoring for bridal outfits, suits, lehengas, and casual attire
  • Unique modern and traditional hand embroidery designs
  • Intricate maggam work for saree borders, blouses, and more
  • Custom embroidered logos for corporate branding

With over 10 years of experience and a 5.0-star rating, our skilled artisans are committed to delivering excellence in every stitch. We take pride in transforming your vision into stunning reality through our expertise in fashion design and tailoring.
